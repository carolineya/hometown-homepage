Visit Fort Collins, CO

Enjoy fun local shops and restaurants in the beautiful foothills of Colorado.

Top three activities to do in Fort Collins:

Visit popular local landmarks

Enjoy the sights and shops in Old Town Square or go for a swim in the City Park Pool.

Explore local restaurants

Visit Coopersmith's for delicious food or a game of pool, and then head over to Walrus Ice Cream to try their Blue Moon flavor.

View the natural beauty of Colorado

Go hiking in Rocky Mountain National Park or the Pawnee National Grasslands.
